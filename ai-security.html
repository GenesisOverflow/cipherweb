AI Security Services

As AI becomes embedded in critical systems, it introduces new threat surfaces. Our AI Security services are designed to help organizations protect AI systems, ensure trustworthy operations, and leverage AI to strengthen their cyber defense posture.

Key Services
  • Securing AI Use: We assess AI architectures, data pipelines, API endpoints, and model deployments to identify weaknesses in confidentiality, integrity, and availability. This ensures secure, ethical, and compliant AI implementation.
  • AI Red Teaming: Our adversarial testing uncovers model-specific vulnerabilities such as prompt injection, data poisoning, model inversion, evasion, and bias. We test both LLMs and traditional ML models against real-world threats.
