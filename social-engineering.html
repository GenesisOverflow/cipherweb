Social Engineering

Testing the Human Element Through Simulated Deception

Humans are often the weakest link in cybersecurity. We replicate real-world social engineering attacks to identify behavioral vulnerabilities and improve awareness.

Key Services
  • Phishing Campaigns: Targeted and mass campaigns simulate credential harvesting, malware delivery, or BEC-style attacks.
  • Pretexting and Impersonation: Our operators pose as internal staff, vendors, or third parties to manipulate targets into divulging sensitive information.
  • Physical Intrusion Testing: We attempt unauthorized access to facilities, including tailgating, badge cloning, and USB drops, to test physical security and human vigilance.
  • Vishing and Smishing: Voice- and SMS-based social engineering campaigns test susceptibility to manipulation via mobile and verbal communication.
  • Awareness Metrics & Reporting: Comprehensive reports include open rates, click-throughs, response times, and improvement recommendations.
